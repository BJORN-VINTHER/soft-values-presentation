Example of a good team

Team members:

  1. Know each other's strength and how to make most use of it
  2. Trust and empowers each other
  3. Can joke around and enjoy each others company
  4. Will share and improve ideas through constructive feedback
  5. Believing that collaboration with a team mate will be faster give a better result

Bad

Team members:

  1. blame other people when issues arrise
  2. Show little interest in the work and discussions
  3. have little interest and knowlegde about the other team members
  4. avoid adressing issues directlyMinimal communication or interest
