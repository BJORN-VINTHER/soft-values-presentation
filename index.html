Example of a good team

  • Team members know each other's strengths and how to make most use of them
  • Team members trust and empowers each other
  • Team members can joke around and enjoy each others company
  • Team members will share and improve ideas through constructive feedback

Bad

Team members:

  • blame other people when issues arrise
  • Show little interest in the work and discussions
  • have little interest and knowlegde about the other team members
  • avoid adressing issues directlyMinimal communication or interest
